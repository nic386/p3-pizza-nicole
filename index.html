A Website About Vehicles.. Why?


I have always said you can tell a lot about a person by what they drive. Whether it be their personality, preferences, values, maybe something they are going through at that time in their life and much more. This site explains some of the vehicles I have owned over the years and where I was in that point in my life to have them. They have all helped me grow into who I am today.
